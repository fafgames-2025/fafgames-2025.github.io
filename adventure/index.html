adventure games

Adventure games are a type of video game. They are about stories and finding solutions to challenges. These games are not fast, they let you enjoy the story and the world in the game. In these games, you play as the main character. You go to different places, meet other characters, and solve puzzles. Solving puzzles helps you move forward in the game. The story is important in adventure games. They are like interactive books or movies. You get to decide what happens in the story. You can play adventure games on computers, game consoles, or phones. They can be simple with just text, or they can have beautiful graphics. Adventure games are fun because they make you think and they tell you a story. If you like mysteries or exploring, you might like adventure games.

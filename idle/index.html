    idle games

    Idle games, often known as incremental or clicker games, are a genre where the gameplay primarily involves the player performing simple actions like clicking to earn in-game currency. As the game progresses, this currency can be used to purchase upgrades that automate these actions, allowing the game to continue even when the player is not actively engaged. They offer a low-pressure gaming experience with a focus on growth and achievement over time.
